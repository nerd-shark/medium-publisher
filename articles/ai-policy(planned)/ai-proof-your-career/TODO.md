### Substack Length Research (2026-03-30)
- General sweet spot: 1,500-2,500 words for most newsletters
- Deep dives/essays: 3,000-5,000+ words perform well IF content earns the length
- Key difference from Medium: Substack readers opted in (subscribed), so they tolerate longer reads
- Medium readers discover you via algorithm; Substack readers chose you
- Top Substack writers (e.g., Hannah Ritchie, 56K subs) get 50K-120K reads on substantive posts
- Recommendation: Substack version can go 4,000-5,000 words — add personal anecdotes, deeper data analysis, more case studies, policy connections
- Sources: fastwordcount.com, multiple Substack writer analyses, Hannah Ritchie reflections
