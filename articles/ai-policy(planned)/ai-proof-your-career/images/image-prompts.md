## Prompt 1: Task Erosion Visualization

```

The desk surface is divided into a grid of task tiles — some tiles are intact and solid (representing human tasks), while others are dissolving into streams of glowing blue-cyan digital particles that drift upward and away. The dissolving tiles should show recognizable work icons: a document, a spreadsheet, a code editor, a report. The remaining solid tiles show more abstract, human concepts: a handshake, a lightbulb, a conversation bubble, a compass (representing judgment and direction).

The dissolving effect should be gradual — tiles at different stages of erosion, creating a visual spectrum from fully human to fully automated. Use a color palette of warm amber and cream for the human tiles, cool blue and cyan for the digital dissolution, against a clean white desk surface. The composition should be horizontal (16:9) with a slight perspective tilt. The overall mood should be contemplative and thought-provoking — not dystopian, but honest. Include subtle data visualization patterns in the dissolving particles.
```

**Style**: Editorial illustration, top-down desk view, dissolution effect
**Color Palette**: Warm amber/cream (human), cool blue/cyan (AI), white surface
**Mood**: Contemplative, honest, thought-provoking
