This schedule coordinates the publication of seven technical article series on Medium:

- **Agentic AI Series**: Autonomous agents, multi-agent systems, and the future of AI (concludes Apr 29, 2026)
- **Communication Series**: Technical communication and executive presentation skills (concludes Apr 24, 2026)

### Best Posting Times (EST)

- **Primary**: Tuesday-Thursday, 9:00-10:00 AM
- **Secondary**: Tuesday-Thursday, 12:00-1:00 PM
- **Tertiary**: Tuesday-Thursday, 5:00-6:00 PM

### Schedule Adjustment for Optimal Engagement

Current schedule uses Monday/Wednesday/Friday for consistency, but consider:

- **Option A**: Shift Monday posts to Tuesday for better engagement
- **Option B**: Maintain Monday/Wednesday/Friday for predictable cadence
- **Recommendation**: Test both approaches and measure engagement

### Engagement Tactics

1. **First 2 hours critical**: Respond to all comments immediately
2. **Ask questions**: End posts with engagement questions
3. **Tag sparingly**: Only tag when genuinely relevant
4. **Use emojis strategically**: Visual breaks, not decoration
5. **Keep it scannable**: Bullets, short paragraphs, white space
6. **Include numbers**: Concrete metrics and dollar figures
7. **Tell stories**: Personal experiences and war stories
8. **Be contrarian**: Challenge common beliefs (with data)

## Content Pipeline Management

### Article Status Definitions

- **Published**: Live on Medium
- **Ready**: Final draft, ready to publish
- **In Review**: Draft complete, under review
- **In Progress**: Actively writing
- **Outlined**: Structure defined, not started
- **Planned**: On roadmap, not outlined

### Pipeline Health Indicators

**Healthy Pipeline**:

- 2+ articles "Ready" (2-week buffer)
- 3+ articles "In Review" (3-week buffer)
- 5+ articles "Outlined" (5-week buffer)

**At-Risk Pipeline**:

- <1 article "Ready"
- <2 articles "In Review"
- <3 articles "Outlined"

### Weekly Pipeline Review

Every Friday:

1. Review upcoming 2-week schedule
2. Check article status for next 3 publications
3. Identify blockers or delays
4. Adjust schedule if needed
5. Start outlining articles 4-6 weeks out
