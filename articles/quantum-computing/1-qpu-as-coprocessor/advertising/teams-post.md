## Subject: How quantum hardware actually fits in your infrastructure

Part 1 of the Quantum-Centric Architect series — this one covers the QPU as a coprocessor.

Think of it like the GPU trajectory: specialized hardware for specific workloads, accessed via cloud API, sits alongside your classical compute. Covers the hardware approaches (superconducting, trapped ion, photonic), cloud access models (IBM Quantum, Amazon Braket, Azure Quantum), pricing, and the noise problem that defines the current era.

Practical for anyone evaluating quantum readiness or just curious about how this stuff actually works from an infrastructure perspective.
