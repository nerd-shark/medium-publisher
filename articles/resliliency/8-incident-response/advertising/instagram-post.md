## Caption

3:07 AM. Your phone buzzes. Production is down. 🚨

The industry average time to resolve an incident: 197 minutes.
Elite teams: under 10 minutes.

The difference? The first 10 minutes.

The playbook:
⏱️ 0-1 min: Acknowledge ("I'm looking at this")
🔍 1-3 min: Triage (What's broken? What changed?)
📋 3-5 min: Declare incident, assign roles
🔧 5-10 min: First mitigation (rollback, feature flag, failover)

The goal is NOT root cause. It's stopping the bleeding.

The Incident Commander coordinates. They do NOT debug. The moment the IC starts debugging, nobody is coordinating.

Your 3 AM brain runs at 60%. Runbooks compensate.
