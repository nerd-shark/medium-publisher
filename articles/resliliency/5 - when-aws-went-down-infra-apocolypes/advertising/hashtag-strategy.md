## Hashtag Research Notes

**High-performing tags** (based on similar content):
- #AWS (millions of posts, high engagement)
- #DevOps (millions of posts, active community)
- #CloudArchitecture (hundreds of thousands, technical audience)
- #SRE (growing community, highly engaged)
- #ResilienceEngineering (niche but highly relevant)

**Emerging tags** (growing interest):
- #PlatformEngineering (new role, growing fast)
- #CloudNative (CNCF ecosystem)
- #MultiRegion (specific to article topic)

**Avoid**:
- Generic tags like #Technology, #Innovation (too broad, low engagement)
- Overly specific tags like #AWSUsEast1 (too narrow, no audience)
- Trending tags unrelated to content (appears spammy)

## A/B Testing Strategy

**Test 1**: Technical vs Professional hashtags
- Version A: Heavy on technical (#AWS, #Kubernetes, #DistributedSystems)
- Version B: Heavy on professional (#TechLeadership, #EngineeringLeadership, #SoftwareArchitecture)
- Measure: Engagement rate, audience demographics

**Test 2**: Hashtag count
- Version A: Minimal (5-10 hashtags)
- Version B: Maximum (25-30 hashtags)
- Measure: Reach, engagement rate

**Test 3**: Hashtag placement
- Version A: Hashtags at end of post
- Version B: Hashtags integrated into post text
- Measure: Readability, engagement
