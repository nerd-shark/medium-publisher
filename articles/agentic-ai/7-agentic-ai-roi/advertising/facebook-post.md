79% of organizations are deploying AI agents. The headline ROI figure is 171% average return. Sounds great — until you look at how those numbers are calculated.

Most teams track API costs and call it a day. Some deployments still deliver 5-10x returns. The ones that work pick high-volume, bounded-decision problems where the math works at scale. The ones that fail pick impressive demo problems with thin economics.
