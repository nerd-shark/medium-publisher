# Featured Image Prompts for Multi-Agent Systems Article

## Prompt 1: AI Bot Assembly Line
A clean, modern illustration of AI robot workers collaborating on a futuristic assembly line. Show 5-6 distinct robot workers at different stations along a conveyor belt, each performing a specialized task. First bot (blue) inspects incoming parts with scanning beams, second bot (purple) assembles components with precision arms, third bot (green) welds or connects pieces with light beams, fourth bot (orange) applies finishing touches, fifth bot (teal) performs quality control with holographic displays. Each bot has a unique geometric design with glowing elements and circuit patterns. Data streams and light particles flow between bots showing real-time coordination and communication. The assembly line should have a sleek, high-tech appearance with subtle grid patterns. Products move along the conveyor as glowing objects. Style: clean, industrial-modern, emphasizing workflow and specialization. Background: minimalist factory floor with soft ambient lighting. No text, no humans.

## Prompt 2: AI Bots Playing Basketball
A dynamic, energetic illustration of AI robot players on a basketball court working as a team. Show 5 distinct robot players in action - one bot passing the ball (blue), one receiving (purple), one defending (red), one setting a screen (green), and one preparing to shoot (orange). Each bot has a unique geometric design with glowing elements and circuit patterns. Data streams and light trails show their coordination and communication as they execute a play. The composition should emphasize teamwork, movement, and strategic positioning. Basketball court with subtle tech grid overlay. Style: dynamic, energetic, modern with motion blur effects. No text, no humans, no jerseys with numbers.

## Prompt 3: AI Bot Symphony Conductor View
An artistic overhead view of an AI bot orchestra arranged in a semicircle, as seen from the conductor's perspective. Show 7-8 specialized robot musicians, each with distinct shapes and colors (violins in blue, cellos in purple, brass in orange, woodwinds in green, percussion in teal). The bots are geometric, sleek designs with glowing elements. From the center (conductor's position), flowing ribbons of light and data connect to each bot, representing coordination and communication. Musical notation and data particles float in the air between them. The composition emphasizes the hub-and-spoke coordination pattern. Background: dark concert hall with dramatic lighting focused on the ensemble. Style: artistic, sophisticated, emphasizing structure and harmony. No text, no humans.

## Prompt 4: AI Bot Construction Crew
A dynamic illustration of specialized AI robot workers collaborating to build a modern house. Show 5 distinct construction bots working simultaneously on different parts of the structure: architect bot (blue) holding holographic blueprints and directing the project, carpenter bot (brown/orange) framing walls with precision laser tools, electrician bot (yellow) installing glowing circuit-like wiring, plumber bot (teal) working on pipes that glow with data streams, and roofing bot (red) on top installing solar panel-like roof elements. Each bot has a unique geometric design matching their specialty, with glowing elements and tool attachments. The house is partially constructed showing the framework, with data streams and light particles flowing between bots representing coordination and communication. The composition should show the house from a 3/4 angle to see multiple bots working. Style: clean, modern, industrial with a sense of organized collaboration. Background: construction site with subtle tech grid overlay. No text, no humans, no safety vests or hard hats.

## Usage Notes
- All prompts designed for 16:9 landscape format (ideal for Medium featured images)
- Emphasize: no text, no labels, no human figures
- Focus on: teamwork, coordination, specialization, and harmony
- Visual metaphors: orchestra (hierarchical coordination), basketball team (peer collaboration)
- Each bot has unique design/color representing different specializations
- Data streams and light show communication between agents
