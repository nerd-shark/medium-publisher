## Post

Training GPT-3 emitted 552 tons of CO₂ 🚗🚗🚗🚗🚗

That's like driving 5 cars for their entire lifetime.

AI has a massive carbon problem. But you can train models efficiently.

**Model Efficiency**:
• Pruning: 90% smaller, 95% accuracy
• Quantization: FP32→INT8 = 4x speedup
• Distillation: DistilBERT is 40% smaller, 60% faster

**Training Optimization**:
• Mixed precision: 2x faster (3 lines of code)
• Early stopping: save 20-40% compute
• Efficient batch sizes

**Carbon-Aware Training**:
• Schedule during low-carbon hours (16% reduction)
• Use renewable regions (30x difference!)
• Pause/resume based on grid intensity

**Inference Optimization**:
• Batch inference: 10-100x more efficient
• Dynamic batching: 10ms latency for 10-100x throughput
• Model caching: 80% hit rate = 80% less compute

Real example: Team switched from scheduled retraining to performance-based. 80% reduction in compute. Same performance.

The tradeoff: 95% accuracy with 10x efficiency > 98% accuracy with 1x efficiency
