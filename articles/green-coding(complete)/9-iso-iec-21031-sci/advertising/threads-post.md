Nine articles on green coding. Most focused on reducing carbon. Few gave me a consistent number to track.

ISO/IEC 21031 fixes that. One equation: SCI = (E × I + M) per R. Energy, carbon intensity, embodied emissions, per functional unit.

It's a rate, not a total. Offsets don't count. Accenture measured 0.025 gCO₂ per API call. Autostrade saved 15% across 60 apps.
