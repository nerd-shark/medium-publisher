## Step-by-Step Setup Instructions

1. **Go to Beacons.ai** and sign up (free account)
2. **Choose "Creator" profile type**
3. **Set up profile:**

   - Upload profile photo
   - Add name and headline
   - Write bio (use text above)
   - Add social media links
4. **Add links section by section:**

   - Create "Green Coding Series" section
   - Add article links with descriptions
   - Create "Connect With Me" section
   - Add social profiles
   - Create "More" section
   - Add additional links
5. **Customize design:**

   - Choose theme (dark with green accents recommended)
   - Adjust button styles
   - Set fonts (clean, readable)
   - Preview on mobile
6. **Enable features:**

   - Turn on analytics
   - Enable email capture (optional)
   - Set up custom domain (optional, paid feature)
7. **Get your Beacons URL:**

   - Will be: beacons.ai/[your-username]
   - Add this to your Instagram bio
   - Add to other social profiles
8. **Update regularly:**

   - Add new articles as you publish
   - Update "Coming Soon" placeholders
   - Check analytics monthly

## Pro Tips

1. **Keep it updated**: Nothing worse than "Coming Soon" links that never come
2. **Use emojis**: Makes links more scannable and engaging
3. **Short descriptions**: 1-2 sentences max per link
4. **Mobile-first**: Most Instagram users are on mobile
5. **Track clicks**: Use Beacons analytics to see what resonates
6. **Consistent branding**: Match your Instagram aesthetic
7. **Test on mobile**: Always preview before publishing
8. **Series strategy**: Start with 1-2 series, expand as you publish more
9. **Reorder sections**: Put most active series at top
10. **Cross-promote**: Mention other series at end of each article

## Content Strategy Across Series

**Publishing cadence:**
- Week 1: Green Coding article
- Week 2: Communication/Leadership article
- Week 3: Architecture/DevOps article
- Week 4: Resilient Systems article
- Rotate to keep variety

**Cross-promotion:**
- End each article with "Other series I'm writing:"
- Link between related topics (e.g., Green Coding → Architecture)
- Create "mega articles" that combine series (e.g., "Resilient + Green Architecture")

**Series completion:**
- Aim for 4-6 articles per series
- Mark series as "Complete" or "Ongoing"
- Create "Series Index" article for each completed series
