### Tweet 3/6
Most backend services are I/O-bound.

80-95% of time = waiting on databases and network calls.

Language efficiency only matters for the 5-20% that's actually computing.

Your 75x benchmark advantage → 2-5x real-world advantage.

### Tweet 4/6
Memory management is the hidden energy tax:

🦀 Rust: Zero GC overhead, C-level performance
☕ Java: JVM eats 200-500MB just to exist
🐹 Go: Sub-ms GC pauses, 5-10% CPU overhead
🐍 Python: GIL means one thread at a time

Discord migrated Go→Rust for this exact reason.

For long-running services? Profile first.
