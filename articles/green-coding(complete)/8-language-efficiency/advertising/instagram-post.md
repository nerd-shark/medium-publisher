## Caption

C is 75x more energy-efficient than Python 🔋

Your Rust evangelist swears a rewrite would save $38K/year. The benchmarks are real. The context is missing.

**The Efficiency Spectrum** (normalized to C = 1.0):

🟢 Native Compiled:
• C: 1.00x energy
• Rust: 1.03x energy
• C++: 1.34x energy

🔵 JIT/Managed:
• Java: 1.98x energy
• Go: 3.23x energy
• JavaScript: 4.45x energy

🔴 Interpreted:
• Python: 75.88x energy
• PHP: 29.30x energy
• Ruby: 69.91x energy

**But here's the catch** 👀

These benchmarks measure pure computation. Most backend services spend 80-95% of time waiting on I/O. Your 75x advantage becomes 2-5x in production.

**What actually matters:**

⚡ Memory management: Discord migrated Go → Rust because GC caused latency spikes every 2 minutes

🔄 Static vs dynamic typing: Java field access = 1ns. Python = 100ns. 100x difference per operation.

☁️ Lambda cold starts: Rust ~50ms vs Java ~3,000ms (60x!)

**The honest math:**

Rewrite = 4 engineers × 6 months = $340K
Annual savings = $38K
Break-even = 8.7 years 😬

**The smart approach:**

Python orchestrates. Rust/C does the heavy lifting. NumPy runs within 5% of pure C performance.

Profile before you rewrite. The language matters less than you think. The algorithm matters more. The architecture matters most.
