## Hashtag Research Notes

### High-Performing Hashtags (Based on Engagement)
1. #DevOps - Consistently high engagement across all platforms
2. #SoftwareEngineering - Broad reach, professional audience
3. #CloudComputing - Growing interest, technical audience
4. #Sustainability - Broad appeal, trending topic
5. #Docker - Specific technical audience, high engagement

### Emerging Hashtags (Watch for Growth)
1. #GreenCoding - New but growing rapidly
2. #SustainableCI - Very niche but highly targeted
3. #PlatformEngineering - Emerging role/discipline
4. #CloudOptimization - Cost focus resonates with decision makers
5. #TechForGood - Positive framing for sustainability

### Hashtags to Avoid
1. #Code - Too generic, low engagement
2. #Tech - Too broad, gets lost in noise
3. #Software - Too generic
4. #Green - Too broad (not tech-specific)
5. #Environment - Too broad (not tech-specific)
