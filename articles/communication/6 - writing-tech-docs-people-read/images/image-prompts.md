# Featured Image Prompts — Writing Technical Documents

## Prompt 1: The Layered Document
A clean, modern illustration showing a large technical document splitting into three distinct layers floating in space. The top layer is a single glowing page (gold/amber) labeled-style with minimal content — representing the executive summary. The middle layer is a small stack of 3-4 pages (blue) with diagrams and charts visible — the technical overview. The bottom layer is a thick stack of pages (gray/silver) with dense text and code — the implementation details. Three distinct silhouette figures stand at different heights: one at the top reaching for the gold page, one in the middle examining the blue pages, one at the bottom reading the full stack. Light beams connect the layers showing they're part of one document. Style: clean, modern, conceptual with soft gradients. Dark background with glowing document elements. 16:9 landscape. No text labels, no real words on pages.

## Prompt 2: The Unread RFC
A dramatic illustration of a massive, towering stack of paper documents (hundreds of pages) sitting alone on a sleek modern desk. The stack glows faintly with technical diagrams visible on the pages. Around the stack, multiple empty chairs are pushed back from the desk — everyone has left. A single small sticky note sits on top of the stack. Dust particles float in dramatic light beams coming from a window. In contrast, on the opposite side of the desk, a single glowing page (bright, warm light) has three figures gathered around it, engaged and pointing at it. The contrast between the ignored massive document and the engaged single page is the focal point. Style: cinematic, dramatic lighting, contrast between abandonment and engagement. 16:9 landscape. No readable text, no real words.

## Prompt 3: The Inverted Pyramid
An architectural illustration of a large inverted pyramid (point down) made of translucent glass or crystal, floating above a modern workspace. The top (widest) section glows bright gold — representing the most important information (conclusion, recommendation). The middle section glows blue — supporting evidence. The narrow bottom point glows soft gray — background context. Light flows from top to bottom, getting dimmer. Around the pyramid, abstract representations of readers: many figures clustered at the top level, fewer at the middle, very few at the bottom — showing how people actually read. Geometric data visualization elements float around the pyramid. Style: architectural, clean, modern with glass/crystal aesthetic. Dark background. 16:9 landscape. No text, no labels.

## Prompt 4: The Three Audiences
A split-scene illustration showing three distinct reading environments side by side, separated by thin glowing dividers. Left panel: an executive figure at a standing desk, glancing at a single glowing page for 10 seconds — a clock shows minimal time. Middle panel: a product manager figure at a desk with 3-5 pages spread out, highlighting sections with a digital pen. Right panel: an engineer figure surrounded by stacks of documents, multiple screens showing code and diagrams, deeply immersed. Each panel has a different color temperature — warm gold (executive), cool blue (product), green (engineering). The same document appears in all three panels but at different depths. Style: modern, triptych composition, distinct color zones. 16:9 landscape. No text, no readable content.

---

## Usage Notes
- All prompts designed for 16:9 landscape format (ideal for Medium featured images)
- No text, no labels, no readable words on documents
- Focus on: document layers, reading patterns, audience differences, information hierarchy
- Recommended: Prompt 1 (Layered Document) or Prompt 2 (Unread RFC) for featured image
