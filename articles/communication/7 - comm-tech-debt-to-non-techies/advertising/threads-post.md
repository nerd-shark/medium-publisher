18 months ago: new endpoint = 3 days. Today: 2 weeks. Same team.

Every time someone says "refactoring" the conversation dies.

What worked: "We took a shortcut 6 months ago. Saved 2 weeks. Since then, every payment feature costs 3 extra days. 14 features × 3 days = $84K."

"We're paying $84K interest on a $20K loan."

VP got it immediately. Not because I explained the code. Because I explained the money.

Don't ask to refactor. Ask to invest in velocity.
