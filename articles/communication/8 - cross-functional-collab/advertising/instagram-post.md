## Caption

3 months of work. Beautiful design. Impressive engineering. Launch day: engagement dropped 12%. 📉

Everyone did their job. The product still failed.

The problem: three disciplines working in parallel instead of together.

Engineering thinks in systems 🔧
Product thinks in outcomes 📊
Design thinks in experience 🎨

Same meeting. Three languages. Three definitions of "done."

How to bridge the gap:
→ Translate constraints into business impact
→ Use "Yes, and" instead of "No"
→ Offer options, not ultimatums
→ Show up to their world

The engineers who advance fastest aren't the best coders. They're the ones who multiply their impact through others.

This is the finale of my 8-part Communication series. Communication is a technical skill — it has patterns, anti-patterns, and best practices.
