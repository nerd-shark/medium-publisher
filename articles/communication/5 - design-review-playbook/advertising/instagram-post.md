# Instagram Post - Design Review Playbook

## Post

Your 2-hour design review just wasted $1,800 and made zero decisions. 😤

The problem? No facilitator. Just advocates arguing.

After facilitating 100+ design reviews, here's what works:

🎯 4 TYPES OF DESIGN REVIEWS
• Requirements DR: Validate the problem
• Preliminary DR: Validate architecture
• Detailed DR: Validate implementation
• Final DR: Validate production readiness

Match reviews to risk. High-risk = all 4. Low-risk = 1.

🛑 5 PATTERNS THAT KILL REVIEWS
1. Bikeshedding (debating trivial details)
2. Loud voices (hierarchy kills feedback)
3. Analysis paralysis (endless research)
4. Design by committee (Frankenstein compromises)
5. Missing context (reviewers don't know history)

⚖️ HOW TO FACILITATE
• Stay neutral (even if you have opinions)
• Draw out quiet participants
• Time-box discussions
• Use parking lot for tangents
• Drive to decisions
• Document in ADRs within 24 hours

📋 DECISION FRAMEWORKS
Consensus = Everyone agrees (rare)
Consent = No blocking objections (practical)
DACI = Clear roles (high-stakes)

Most reviews work best with consent.

The hardest part? Being BOTH presenter and facilitator.

Solution: Explicitly separate the roles. "I'm in facilitator mode now."
