# Medium Article Writing Workspace

## Purpose

Content creation workspace for technical Medium articles across multiple topic series. Manages article development, versioning, and social media advertising materials.

## Directory Structure

### Topic Series Directories

**Note**: This list will grow over time as new topic series are added.

**Current Series**:
- `/agentic-ai/` - AI agents and agentic systems articles
- `/communication/` - Technical communication and leadership articles
- `/green-coding/` - Sustainable software engineering series
- `/resilience/` - System resilience and reliability articles
- `/risk-management-stuff/` - Risk management content
- `/coverage/` - Additional topic coverage (placeholder)

**Future Series**: New directories will be created as additional topic areas are developed

### Root-Level Documents
- `ARTICLE-WRITING-PROCESS.md` - Complete article development workflow
- `ADVERTISING-BACKFILL-COMPLETE.md` - Advertising material completion tracking
- `MEDIUM-ARTICLE-RELEASE-SCHEDULE.md` - Publication calendar
- `published.md` - Published articles tracking
- `PUBLISHED-ARTICLES-UPDATE-REQUIREMENTS.md` - Post-publication update guidelines
- `UNPUBLISHED-ARTICLES-UPDATE-REQUIREMENTS.md` - Pre-publication checklist
- `WEEK-1-ARTICLES-SUMMARY.md` - Weekly progress tracking

## Article Structure Pattern

Each article follows this structure:
```
{topic}/{N}-{article-name}/
├── versions/
│   ├── v1-{article-name}.md
│   ├── v2-{article-name}.md
│   └── v{N}-{article-name}.md
├── advertising/
│   ├── linkedin-post.md
│   ├── x-post.md
│   ├── instagram-post.md
│   ├── facebook-post.md
│   ├── threads-post.md
│   ├── reddit-post.md
│   ├── teams-post.md
│   └── hashtag-strategy.md
├── images/
├── {article-name}-proposal.md (optional)
└── TODO.md
```

## Content Development Workflow

1. **Pre-Writing**: Create directory structure, review writing samples
2. **Proposal**: Define structure and talking points (optional)
3. **Version 1**: Initial complete draft
4. **Advertising**: Create platform-specific promotional materials
5. **Iteration**: Refine through versioned updates (v2, v3, etc.)
6. **Publication**: Final review and Medium publication
7. **Tracking**: Update published.md and release schedule

## Key Principles

- **Version Control**: Never modify existing versions, always create new
- **Iterative Refinement**: Each version adds/expands/refines content
- **Advertising Sync**: Update promotional materials with each major version
- **Series Continuity**: Link articles within series for navigation
- **SEO Optimization**: Keywords, tags, and internal linking mandatory
- **Tone**: Conversational but authoritative, practical examples with real numbers
